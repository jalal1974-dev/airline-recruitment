ISSUE 1: No document upload section.

multi-file upload section. The applicant should be able to upload

1. Resume/CV (السيرة الذاتية):
   - Label: "السيرة الذاتية *" / "Resume/CV *"

   - Required field
   
2. Professional Certificates (الشهادات المهنية):
   - Label: "الشهادات المهنية" / "Professional Certificates"
   - Accept: .pdf,.doc,.docx,.jpg,.jpeg,.png
   - MULTIPLE files allowed (can upload several certificates)
   - Optional field
   
3. Educational Certificates (الشهادات التعليمية):
   - Label: "الشهادات التعليمية" / "Educational Certificates"
   - Accept: .pdf,.doc,.docx,.jpg,.jpeg,.png

   - Optional field

   - Label: "وثائق أخرى (جواز سفر، هوية، الخ)" / "Other Documents (passport, ID, etc.)"
   - Accept: .pdf,.doc,.docx,.jpg,.jpeg,.png
   - MULTIPLE files allowed
   - Optional field
